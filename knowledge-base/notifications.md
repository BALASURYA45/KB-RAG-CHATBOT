# Notifications

## Manage Email Notifications

To manage email notifications, open Settings and select Notifications. Turn individual notification types on or off, then save the changes.

Critical security and billing messages may still be sent even when optional notifications are disabled.

## Stop Marketing Emails

To stop marketing emails, open Settings, select Notifications, and turn off Product Updates and Promotions. You can also use the unsubscribe link at the bottom of a marketing email.

Transactional emails such as invoices, password resets, and security alerts cannot be disabled.

## Notification Not Received

If you did not receive a notification, check the email address on your profile and review spam or junk folders. Ask your email administrator to allow messages from the application domain.

For password reset emails, request a new link if the previous message is older than 30 minutes.
