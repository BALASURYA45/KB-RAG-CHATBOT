# Privacy

## Request Data Deletion

To request deletion of personal data, open Account Settings, select Privacy, and choose Request Data Deletion. Confirm the request and provide any required details.

Some records, such as invoices and audit logs, may be retained for legal or security reasons.

## Download Privacy Report

To download a privacy report, open Account Settings, select Privacy, and choose Download Privacy Report.

The report includes profile details, workspace memberships, and recent privacy-related account actions.

## Update Communication Preferences

To update communication preferences, open Settings, select Privacy, and choose Communication Preferences.

Security, billing, and account ownership messages are required and cannot be disabled.
