# Billing

## Update Payment Method

To update your payment method, go to Billing, choose Payment Methods, and select Add Payment Method. Make the new card the default payment method before removing an old card.

## Download Invoice

Invoices are available from Billing under Invoice History. Select the invoice month and choose Download PDF.

## Failed Payment

If a payment fails, update the default payment method and retry the payment from Billing. Access may be limited until the payment succeeds.
