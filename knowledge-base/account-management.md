# Account Management

## Change Email Address

To change your email address, open Account Settings and select Profile. Enter the new email address and confirm it using the verification email.

## Close Account

Account closure requests must be submitted from Account Settings under Close Account. The account owner must confirm the request before the account is queued for closure.

## Add Team Member

To add a team member, open Team Settings, select Invite Member, enter the email address, and choose the correct role.
