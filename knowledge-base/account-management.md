# Account Management

## Change Email Address

To change your email address, open Account Settings and select Profile. Enter the new email address and confirm it using the verification email.

After the new email address is verified, future sign-in alerts, billing notices, and password reset messages are sent to the new address. If you do not receive the verification email, check spam folders and confirm that the new address was typed correctly.

## Close Account

Account closure requests must be submitted from Account Settings under Close Account. The account owner must confirm the request before the account is queued for closure.

Before closing an account, download required invoices, export important data, and remove active team members. Account closure does not automatically refund recent charges.

## Add Team Member

To add a team member, open Team Settings, select Invite Member, enter the email address, and choose the correct role.

Invited users receive an email invitation. If the invitation expires, open Team Settings, find the pending invite, and choose Resend Invitation.

## Remove Team Member

To remove a team member, open Team Settings, select Members, choose the user, and click Remove Member. Confirm the removal before saving.

Removed users immediately lose access to shared workspaces. Content created by the removed user remains available to account owners and admins.

## Change Account Owner

Only the current account owner can transfer ownership. Open Account Settings, select Ownership, choose a new owner from active admins, and confirm the transfer.

The new owner receives billing, security, and account closure permissions. Review admin roles before transferring ownership.
