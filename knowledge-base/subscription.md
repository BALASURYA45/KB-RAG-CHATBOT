# Subscription

## Cancel Subscription

To cancel your subscription, open Billing, select Subscription, and choose Cancel Subscription. Review the cancellation details and confirm the request.

After cancellation, paid features remain available until the end of the current billing period. The account is not deleted when a subscription is canceled.

## Change Plan

To change your plan, open Billing and select Subscription. Choose Change Plan, compare the available plans, and confirm the new plan.

Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades usually take effect at the start of the next billing period.

## Renew Subscription

To renew an expired subscription, open Billing, select Subscription, and choose Renew. Confirm the payment method and complete the checkout.

If renewal fails, update the default payment method and try again.

## Trial Expiration

When a trial expires, access may be limited until a paid plan is selected. Open Billing, choose a plan, and add a valid payment method to continue using paid features.
