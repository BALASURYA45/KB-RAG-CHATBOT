# Password Reset

## Reset Password

To reset your password, open Settings, select Security, and choose Reset Password. Enter your current password, then type and confirm the new password.

After saving, sign out of all active sessions and sign back in with the new password.

## Forgot Password

If you forgot your password, select Forgot Password on the sign-in page. Enter the email address on your account and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.

Reset links expire after 30 minutes. Request a new link if the previous one expired.
